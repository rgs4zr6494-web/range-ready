# Range Ready — GitHub Pages Setup Guide
## 3 steps. Free. Done in under 5 minutes.

---

## STEP 1 — Create a free GitHub account

1. Go to **https://github.com**
2. Click **Sign up** (top right)
3. Choose a username — something simple like `rangeready` or anything you like
4. Use any email address, create a password
5. Verify your email when they send you the link

---

## STEP 2 — Create the repository (your website's home)

1. Once logged in, click the **+** button (top right) → **New repository**
2. Name it exactly: **`range-ready`**
3. Make sure **Public** is selected
4. Check the box: ✅ **Add a README file**
5. Click **Create repository**

---

## STEP 3 — Upload your files

1. Inside your new repository, click **Add file** → **Upload files**
2. Drag and drop ALL THREE files from your Golf folder:
   - `index.html`
   - `morning-flexibility.html`
   - *(future workouts go here too — just drag and drop)*
3. Scroll down, click **Commit changes**

### Turn on GitHub Pages:
1. Click **Settings** (tab at the top of the repo)
2. Click **Pages** in the left sidebar
3. Under "Branch" — select **main**, folder stays as **/ (root)**
4. Click **Save**
5. Wait 2-3 minutes

### Your site is live at:
**`https://YOUR-USERNAME.github.io/range-ready`**

Bookmark that on your iPhone and you're done.

---

## Using on your iPhone + Vizio TV

**AirPlay (easiest):**
1. Open the URL in Safari on your iPhone
2. Swipe down from top-right corner → tap **Screen Mirroring**
3. Select your Vizio TV from the list
4. Done — full screen on your TV

**If AirPlay doesn't appear:**
- Your Vizio TV must be on the same WiFi network as your iPhone
- On the Vizio TV remote, press **V** or **Home** → SmartCast must be running
- Some older Vizio models need the SmartCast app on your phone instead

---

## Adding new workouts later

When a new workout is ready:
1. Go to **github.com/YOUR-USERNAME/range-ready**
2. Click **Add file** → **Upload files**
3. Drop in the new HTML file
4. Click **Commit changes**
5. Live in ~1 minute

That's it — the home screen will automatically show it as an option.
